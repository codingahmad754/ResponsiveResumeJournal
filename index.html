Trials and Tribulations of a self taught developer.

It started when my sister who works for Cloudflare asked me if I would take advantage of free coding courses provided by her company. My current career wasn't the most thrilling and I was ready to take a leap of faith into the tech world.

How I stay commited to learning

I've always been into computer gaming so I assumed that coding wouldn't be too different. Boy, was I wrong! Just centering a div or an image was turning out to be a headache but I persevered. I adopted the mindset that the answers are all on the net I just had to look for them. I'm a little over a year into my developer journey and the things I've not only learned but retained amazes me everytime I run into a coding problem. I have so many different possible solutions that race to my mind when I used to draw a blank.
